SHAWARMA

Happiness is eating a good Shawarma.

Enjoy Eating!!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Marinade
  • Spices
  • Flat bread
  • Vegetables
  • Sauces
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Season and marinate the meat.
  2. Thinly slice the marinated meat.
  3. Grill the meat until cooked.
  4. Heat flatbread.
  5. Place meat and toppings on flatbread.
  6. Roll it up.
  7. Enjoy hot.
