Pizza

Happiness is a slice of pizza.

Enjoy Eating!!

Ingredients

  • PIizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice(e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers)
  • Olive oil
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475-500°F (245-260°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough into desired shape and thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce on dough, leaving a border. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  4. Transfer pizza to preheated oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with herbs (optional), slice, and serve hot.
