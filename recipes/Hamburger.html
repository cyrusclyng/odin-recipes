HAMBURGER

Happiness is eating a juicy hamburger.

Enjoy Eating!!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into patties.
  2. Sprinkle patties with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill or pan-fry patties until cooked to your liking.
  4. Toast hamburger buns on the grill or in a pan.
  5. Place cooked patties on the bottom half of the bun.
  6. Layer lettuce, tomato, onion, and cheese on top of the patty.
  7. Spread ketchup and mustard on the top half of the bun.
  8. Place the top half of the bun over the toppings to finish.
  9. Ready to serve
